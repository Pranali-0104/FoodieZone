FAQ's

About our website

The FoodieZone website helps you is part of Learning and has a mission to teach and encourage people to cook more. The site primarily publishes recipes from television programmes, but sometimes commissions original recipes to accompany public service campaigns.

How long does it take for my food to be delivered?

Our estimated delivery time is usually mentioned on the website or app, and it varies depending on the distance between the restaurant and your location. On average, we deliver within 30-45 minutes of placing your order.

What if I receive the wrong order or there is something missing from my order?

We apologize for any inconvenience caused. In the unlikely event that you receive the wrong order or something is missing from your order, please contact our customer service team immediately, and we will resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

Are there any delivery charges?

We may have a minimum order value for free delivery, and if your order falls below that value, there may be a delivery charge. The delivery charges vary based on the location and the restaurant you are ordering from. You can check the delivery charges on the website or app before placing your order.

What payment options are available for my order?

We offer various payment options, such as online payments via credit/debit cards, net banking, digital wallets, and cash on delivery (COD) in some locations. You can choose the payment method that suits you best while placing your order. Please note that COD may not be available for all orders and locations.
